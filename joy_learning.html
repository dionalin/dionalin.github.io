Joy & Learning
Cleaning Set for Children

Overview

Joy & Learning is a cleaning set designed for children doing their daily or weekly chores. The aim of this cleaning set is to delight kids and help develop good cleaning manners while children are young. Instead of considering children as abstract thinkers in cleaning, the concept behind this cleaning set is to break the task down into a sequence children can follow, and accept the results being less than the "perfect" job an adult would do.

Problems

It is a difficult lesson for parents to establish their children’s good cleaning habits, although they know that doing chore can teach children self-discipline, enhance their self-esteem and build a sense of pride through achievement.
In some cases, children will refuse the delegation of doing chores from parents. Sometimes, parents will do the chore themselves and this compromise always causes the problem of character development and the establishment of good manner in their daily lives.

Strategy - The Learning Motivation

We draw a conclusion from a series of observations and tests that the easiest method to make children do it themselves is to apply story connection and design with their rich imagination. At the same time, forms mimicking sea animals can also increase impression and intuitive response for children.

User research

We also conducted several interesting brainstorming sessions with the kids, in order to understanding how they think of the cleaning tools by their imaginations. These graffiti considerably inspired us and helped us elaborate the design language of the products, which is also the reference along the whole process of iterations: sketched, mockups, the final design, and the story for the commercialization.

Design Highlight

I. Sweeping learning

When we sweep, we gather the dirt into a target area, but the target is abstract. Using the U-shaped guidance as auxiliary which is removable from the bottom of the dustpan, children learn how to sweep into a target area. As children develops more confidence and skill in wielding the broom, children can gradually understand how to use the broom and dustpan together.

II. Mopping learning

The octopus like mop makes wringing a mop an interesting task. The auxiliary rope at the bottom of the bucket can be pulled out for defining the target area which makes the learning of mopping the floor easier. Carved scale inside the bucket is easily visible from above when children fill the bucket with water. These guidances are useful for breaking the task of mopping floor down into a sequence children can follow.

III. Wiping learning

The dotted lines on the front side of the rag help children fold the rag to the correct size and shape before wiping. On the back side of the rag, the graphics on the rag are designed to stimulate children’s imagination while doing chores.

Impact

Getting young children involved with household tasks can be the perfect opportunity to give them a helping role in the family. Contributing to the family in this fashion will teach children self-discipline, enhance their self-esteem and build a sense of pride through achievement.
Joy & Learning allows the children to establish a sense of achievement while enjoying the fun of doing cleanup work. Children can build up their responsibility and confidence. They will eventually share household cleaning work and develop independence and basic living skills as well as caring for the environment and developing excellent character.
