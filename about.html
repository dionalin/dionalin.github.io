The story of this website

This website is the 3rd version of my portfolio site since the first one in 2013. It is optimized for desktops, tablets and smartphones. Everything here was designed and hand-coded by myself.
My portfolio was originally on squarespace, a solution back then. The templates were beautiful. However, along the way of editing the content on the platform, there're always some layouts or designs I really hope I could customize but being constrained by the defualts. To be able to create beautiful things that 100% realizes what I've designed, one day at the beginning of 2017, I made up my mind, learning coding, and building up my website from scratch.
After 6 months of effort, this website was released on December 30th in 2017. I'm glad to share with you what I've achieved, and I feel very empowered to build up something on my own. Yet, this is just a starting point. Where it shares how my design begins, iterates, succeeds, and fails.
