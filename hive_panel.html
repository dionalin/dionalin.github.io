Hive Admin Panel
Category Management Platform

Overview

For Honestbee grocery business, category management of grocery products is the key to drive sales.
In this project, I worked closely with category managers to define a better way for their category management working flow across countries. I recognised their working flow problems, designed a user friendly interface, and improved their day-to-day working flow.

Impact

The old system was created with bootstrap library without understanding the prioritize of category managers, which resulted in low working efficiency on category management. After the software improvements, posistive feedbacks from our user was constantly received, a sign of success in the improvement of design.

User

Category Manager’s responsibility is to manage categoreis and ensure they make the best decisions that drives sales.

Goal

  1. Provide a simple interface for category managers to manage grocery taxonomy and help them make decisions that drive sales
  2. Making grocery category management more autonomous and efficient across all countries
  3. Enabling product team members to get better insights at every level of the taxonomy across the grocery marketplace

User permission

All the setups on hive will make direct impact on the websites. To better manage the decision making process based on different level of positions, user permmision was proposed, including a regional category manager and local category managers, holding different level of control based on their responsibility scope.

Final thoughts

In most cases, people always put internal tools as the last priority, as long as internal management tool works. The invisible cost - imagine how much the organization spends to hire the talents, but at the end making the talents spending spending hours, days, or weeks, in order, to get a simple task done, is just brutal. Investing in a well-designed internal software is as important as consumer facing applications in a business.
